TYPES OF INDIAN ARMY

1.PARA COMMANDO

para commando

Para (Special Forces) The Paratrooper (Special Forces), or Para (SF), are the special forces of the Indian Army. This unit was created in June 1966 in the aftermath of the 1965 Indo-Pakistani war.

2.MARCOS IN THE INDIAN NAVY

On February 14, 2024, the Marine Commandos, known as MARCOS, celebrated their 38th Raising Day, marking a significant milestone in their distinguished service to the Indian Navy

3. GARUD COMMANDO FORCE (Indian Air Force)

The Garuds were first unveiled as the Indian Air Force's own Commando force on February 6, 2004, when the first batch of 62 'Air Commandos' passed out of training in New Delhi. Role: Contrary to popular perception, Garuds are not an airfield and key assets protection force as its made to believe.
